## I Did Nothing

This week I kept putting off implementing the [bullet]() integration in the game. Between staring sessions at the IDE, I did random things.

I'd been reading a lot about [midjourney](), an AI that generates art from text descriptions. I think having AI generate art is an excellent idea, and a cure for any kind of creative block. On top of that, extrapolating the concepts the AI generates into more practical things is really interesting.

## Dreamstudio

My urge to mess around with Midjourney ended the moment I saw I'd have to do it _on Discord_. Nothing against Discord, I even like it — except when it kills my graphics card driver — but I already knew the things I'd ask the AI to generate would be, at the very least... strange.

So this week a friend sent me the link to [Dreamstudio]().

## I'm Scared

At the end of the day, _Dreamstudio_ is also _Nightmarestudio_ — it depends on who's using it. The app has limited credits, and I'm starting to understand it's not because of hardware limitations — they're probably just afraid of what people will type. It must be worse than moderating Facebook posts. Ok, nothing is worse than that.

I'll leave it here, since I need to go to the store — I drank all the Red Cua I had. Until next time, and watch out for the _killer pineapple clan_.
