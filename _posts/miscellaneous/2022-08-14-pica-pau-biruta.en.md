## Procrastination

Today was a day of pure and simple procrastination: sometimes that's a good thing. To be completely fair to myself, I did work an hour or two in the afternoon, but the rest of the day was just loafing around. I didn't code a thing and spent the time watching YouTube while playing _R-Type_.

I grew up watching these cartoons on TV. I'm not that old — it's just SBT (a Brazilian TV channel) that's been airing the same thing for _seven hundred and thirty-seven years_.

## The Nice-Guy Woody

There are several generations of the Woody Woodpecker cartoon. When I was a kid I liked the "nice guy" version — the one who was always being chased by things like _the heat-seeking missile that goes after anyone who doesn't buy life insurance_. This was an upstanding woodpecker, one with values and character: he was always _the victim_.

>
> _Fells Bargo_

## The Idiot Woody

Much later came the new Woody Woodpecker series, _where he was just an idiot_. Too much of an idiot, to the point of being pointless.

> ![Pointless](/assets/posts/aleatoriedades/1/picapau_idiota.png)
>
> _THIS IS **GARBAGE** THAT DOESN'T... NOTHING. I'M SORRY_

This cartoon was _garbage_, and it came at a time when animated shows were getting dumber by the minute. On top of that, the art and animation were terrible — I refused to subject myself to that trash. Especially since I was already in my twenties when that garbage showed up on free TV here.

> ![What an unjust world!](/assets/posts/aleatoriedades/1/naosoueu.png)
>
> _I just changed the channel, man._

But can it get worse?

## The Wacky Woody

When I was a kid I didn't like this version — probably because I was a dumb kid. I found him too crazy, _he never did the right thing_.

> <p><iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/uch9UU0yjT4" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>
>
> _I am the necessary evil._

It wasn't until I was older that I realized _this is the best version_, the most _roguish_ Woody of them all.

> ![Wing Rationing](/assets/posts/aleatoriedades/1/picapau_merdeiro.png)
>
> _Just causes chaos and still goes to heaven._

This is the incarnation of chaos, the embodiment of evil, mischief for the sake of mischief. He _robs the police_. He calls soda _"fizzy drink"_. He defies authority. _He wears a turtleneck with spats._

> <p><iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/NzeBZt7j0wY" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>
>
> _Rinocetélius._

## Practically a _Sommelier_

If I were writing this post in English everything would be just fine. But the problem is that the character is called pica-pau in Portuguese, and that word has more meanings in that language than the cartoon has episodes.

But we have to acknowledge some things, so the important thing is to be _absolutely certain_ my keyboard battery doesn't die while I write this: after all this analysis, I can say that _I am a fully certified Woody Woodpecker sommelier_.
